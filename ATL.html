ATL & BTL

Ardour is a recognized and trusted Event Management Company,
actively working in every field of event organization.

ATL and BTL marketing are two powerful communication strategies that work together to build visibility, credibility and consumer engagement for brands. While ATL focuses on mass outreach and brand awareness, BTL drives targeted interaction, consumer participation and measurable conversions.

At Ardour, we design and execute integrated ATL & BTL campaigns that bridge reach with relevance. Our strategic approach ensures that brands not only communicate at scale but also build meaningful connections with their audience at the ground level.

We plan, manage and execute ATL placements across television, radio, print and outdoor media, complemented by BTL activations such as product demonstrations, retail promotions, sampling campaigns and experiential events. This integrated methodology delivers stronger brand recall, deeper audience engagement and higher ROI.

We are providing best services for product launching events. We have lots of arrangements and plans to make every product launch successful. We are serving in this field for a long time and know all the norms to plan and finish an amazing product launch function.
