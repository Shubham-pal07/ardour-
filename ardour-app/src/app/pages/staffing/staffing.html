WHAT WE DO?

Ardour is a recognized and trusted Event Management Company.

Event Workforce & Staffing

We provide trained manpower for all types of events including corporate shows, exhibitions, weddings, concerts, festivals, brand activations, and more. Our network includes hosts, ushers, coordinators, promoters, supervisors, and on-ground support staff to ensure smooth execution and guest management.

Technical Crew & Production Support

Our technical staffing solutions include professional sound engineers, lighting technicians, stage crew, riggers, LED & AV operators, and production assistants. With well-trained technical manpower, we ensure flawless backstage operations, quick setup & dismantling, and high-quality event production.

Artist, Talent & Hospitality Staff

We also arrange hospitality teams such as VIP handlers, greenroom managers, artist coordinators, reception staff, brand presenters, and guest relations personnel. Our talent staffing ensures a premium hospitality experience for clients, artists, and guests throughout the event.

Security, Crowd & Logistics Management

For large-scale events, we provide certified security personnel, crowd controllers, bouncers, valet support, parking management staff, logistics coordinators, and emergency standby teams. Our goal is to maintain discipline, safety, and efficient movement of attendees during events.
